…and more

Fly spikes, crumbling ledges, bait blocks, runaway doors. Hundreds of combinations, one promise: no tells.

The deal. Every trap is invisible until it's already killing you. You cannot dodge it the first time — that's the point. You die, you learn the spot, you go again.

The whole gauntlet is identical every run. It's not random and it's not unfair — it's a memory test wearing a smile. Beat it by remembering, not by reflexes you don't have yet.
